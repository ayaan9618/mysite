why this design.

tbh, i just wanted to be different.

in a world full of ai where people want animations, effects, and pixel-perfect templates —
i wanted something simple. something real.
i just want to be unique.

i like design. i love art, anime, sceneries.
but more than that, i like clarity. peace.

most people build flashy portfolios to show off skills and dump their resume online.
i didn’t want that.

i wanted a site that speaks about me.
not a page filled with tools i know or skills i mastered.

if you're here, you either know me — or you're curious.

so this is
just text and thoughts.
